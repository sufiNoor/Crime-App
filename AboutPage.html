About Crime Prevention Application

Here at Paradigm Shift, we strive to push the envelope and roadmap our robust enterprise through a holistic approach. Millennials often find themselves on the runway, moving forward in crime infested areas. Our practice manages to impact younger generations by doing the heavy lifting upon headlighting their crime related fears.

Through the innovation of a crime rate map application, and a real-time crime update feature, Paradigm Shift manages to close-the-loop on unsafe travel throughout Philadelphia. Adoption of a customer-centric approach will ensure profitability through advertisements while symbiotically reducing crime rates by avoidance.
